Sad people have that feeling of abandonment the things which detach them from themselves.

Sad is not an emotion it's a feeling where something's don't workout the way you want them to. Sad people see the world as an empty space where no one is for them to see the pain or understand the feeling resentment. Understand these people don't actually remain sad 24/7 that is just a feeling temporary but gives them the pain for which they have no words to explain.

Psychology behind such aspects is hard to define all we can state is that the people feel understood if a particular video is helping them to feel what they are going through at that moment.
